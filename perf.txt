That’s fine. You don’t have global npm on PATH, but Vaadin already installed its own npm here:

C:\Users\pronz\.vaadin\node\node.exe
C:\Users\pronz\.vaadin\node\node_modules\npm\bin\npm-cli.js
Use it directly:

$node   = "$env:USERPROFILE\.vaadin\node\node.exe"
$npmCli = "$env:USERPROFILE\.vaadin\node\node_modules\npm\bin\npm-cli.js"

& $node -v
& $node $npmCli -v
& $node $npmCli config get registry
& $node $npmCli ping
& $node $npmCli view zstddec version
